Computer Recycling Services

At IWB, we specialize in sustainable electronic waste management by recycling and refurbishing computer components.
Our services include:

  • RAM Recycling: Recovering and repurposing functional RAM modules.
  • Hard Drive Disposal: Secure data destruction and eco-friendly disposal of HDDs.
  • Motherboard Component Recovery: Salvaging and refurbishing valuable motherboard parts.
  • Data Security Services: Ensuring complete and safe data destruction before recycling.
