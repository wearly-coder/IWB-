Welcome to IWB

It is a lesotho company that operates under the supervision of Co-Founder Kenneth which started in 2024. Kenneth partnered with Shadrack,who joined as a second CEO of the company.The mandate of IWB is to recycle computer parts; mainly for RAM, Hard Drive and components of motherboard.

At IWB, we are committed to revolutionizing electronic recycling and data security. Our mission is to repurpose valuable electronic components while ensuring secure and effective data management. Join us in creating a sustainable future.
